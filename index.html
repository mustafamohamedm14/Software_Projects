Family Bakery is a recognized brand with 20 years in the market. It’s known for baking traditional bread with high quality, baking with care and clients always in mind.

Our Location

We have stores all along the main streets and are positioned in the commercial centers across the city. We are proud to guarantee the freshness and quality of our products.
